The order of phases in a Magic the Gathering turn are as shown below. You can click on each phase to get a more detailed description

Beginning Phase
1. untap
2. upkeep
3. draw

Pre-Combat Main Phase
You can play spells and abilities.

Combat Phase
1. Beginning of Combat - Abilities will trigger at this time. Instants and abilities can be cast
2. Declare Attackers - Choose attacking creatures and targets. Instants and abilities can be cast
3. Declare Defenders - Defending player(s) choose creatures to block attacking creatures. Triggered abilities that trigger off attackers being declared trigger.
4. Combat Damage - First and Double Strike calculated first, then damage to blockers and other targets.
5. End of Combat - "At end of combat" effects trigger. All creatures and planeswalkers are removed from combat. "At end of combat" effects end. Abilities and Instants can be cast.

Post-Combat Mainphase
You can play spells and abilities.

Ending Phase
1. End - "At the beginning of the end step" or "At the beginning of the next end step" triggered abilities trigger.
2. Cleanup - The active player discards down to his maximum hand size. All damaged removed and "until end of turn" or "this turn" effects end.
